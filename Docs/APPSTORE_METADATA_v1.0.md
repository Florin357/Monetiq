## English (U.S.)

### App Name
Ypsilon

### Subtitle
Track loans, income & cashflow

### Promotional Text
Manage loans you've lent or borrowed, track income, and see your upcoming payments—all on your device. No account required.

### Description
Ypsilon helps you track loans, income, and upcoming payments in one simple app.

**Loans**
Track money you've lent to others, borrowed from friends, or owe to banks. Add loan details, payment schedules, and mark payments as paid. View overdue payments at a glance.

**Income**
Add income sources with recurring paydays (weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly, or one-time). See upcoming income alongside loan payments for a complete financial picture.

**Dashboard**
View totals for money to receive and to pay. See all upcoming payments in one list, sorted by due date. Quickly identify overdue items.

**Cashflow**
A 30-day chart shows your expected cashflow based on scheduled payments and income. Track net flow over time.

**Calculator**
Calculate loan payments with adjustable interest rates, payment frequency, and loan terms.

**Multi-Currency**
Add loans and income in different currencies. Each entry displays in its original currency.

**Privacy**
All data stays on your device. No account, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your financial information never leaves your phone.

**Key Features**
• Track loans (lent, borrowed, bank credit)
• Manage income sources and paydays
• View upcoming payments in one place
• 30-day cashflow overview
• Mark payments as paid or postpone them
• Overdue detection (calendar-day based)
• Multi-currency support
• On-device data only
• No account required

Ypsilon is designed for anyone who wants a clear view of their personal loans, income, and payment schedule without complexity or privacy concerns.

## 简体中文

### App Name
Ypsilon

### Subtitle
贷款、收入和现金流

### Promotional Text
管理您借出或借入的贷款，跟踪收入并查看即将到来的付款—全部在您的设备上。无需账户。

### Description
Ypsilon 帮助您在一个简单的应用程序中跟踪贷款、收入和即将到来的付款。

**贷款**
跟踪您借给他人的钱、从朋友那里借的钱或欠银行的钱。添加贷款详情、付款计划并将付款标记为已付。一目了然地查看逾期付款。

**收入**
添加具有定期发薪日的收入来源（每周、每月、每季度、每年或一次性）。查看即将到来的收入以及贷款付款，以获得完整的财务状况。

**仪表板**
查看应收和应付款项的总额。在一个列表中查看所有即将到来的付款，按到期日期排序。快速识别逾期项目。

**现金流**
30 天图表根据预定付款和收入显示您的预期现金流。随时间跟踪净流量。

**计算器**
使用可调整的利率、付款频率和贷款条款计算贷款付款。

**多币种**
以不同货币添加贷款和收入。每个条目以其原始货币显示。

**隐私**
所有数据都保留在您的设备上。无账户、无云同步、无跟踪。您的财务信息永远不会离开您的手机。

**主要功能**
• 跟踪贷款（借出、借入、银行信贷）
• 管理收入来源和发薪日
• 在一个地方查看即将到来的付款
• 30 天现金流概览
• 将付款标记为已付或推迟
• 逾期检测（基于日历日）
• 多币种支持
• 仅设备上数据
• 无需账户

Ypsilon 专为任何想要清楚了解其个人贷款、收入和付款时间表而无需复杂性或隐私问题的人设计。

### Keywords
贷款跟踪器,债务,收入,付款,现金流,个人财务,资金管理,贷款,借款,发薪日,预算,财务概览

### What's New
初始版本，包含贷款跟踪、收入管理和现金流概览。
